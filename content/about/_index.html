Chief Information Officer

{{< employer id="fleet-assist-logo" alt="Fleet Assist Logo" src="images/employers/fleet-assist.svg" href="https://www.fleetassist.co.uk/" >}}
Fleet Assist Nov 2009 to Jan 2020 (10 yrs 3 mos)

After studying and developing a specialism in the political use of the internet at university, I decided to pursue a career in technology.

I joined Fleet Assist as a small family business of 10 people with the task of professionalising their IT and security setup. Over the next 10 years we grew it into a 50 employee multi-million revenue provider of services to the automotive financial services industry.

Initially I performed all development, data and IT roles. As we grew the business and built my teams, the role evolved into an executive who led the technology vision for the company using the broad range of technical skills gained from the early growth phase.

  • Software Development: Developed from a greenfield project, code reviewed and project managed the web based "Atlas" system which was the company's primary system.
  • Data Analytics: Designed and built a mulit-award winning (Fleet News and Alphabet) data analytics platform to process over £2bn in automotive maintenance data.
  • IT: Created and managed an on-prem/cloud hybrid Hyper-V based Windows Server environment within a decommissioned bank vault and Azure.
  • People: Line managed 6 direct reports, with a team of 10 in total across the IT, Software Development, Analytics, Data Administration and Rental Finance departments.
  • Mentorship: Shepherded technical staff through multi year apprenticeships and industry certification programs as well as acting as a guide for young staff.
  • Networking: Procured, configured and monitored a fully redundant network using Juniper firewalls and switches combined with Ubiquiti access points.
  • Telephony: Scaled an Avaya PABX, call reporting and recording system from a 1 to 20+ seat call centre.
  • Security: Adhered to security best practices which were regularly tested through passing IT security audits from our FCA regulated customer base.
  • Procurement: Completed multiple IT technology refreshes within budgets ranging from £1000 up to £100k
  • Facilities: Managed the challenging environment of a 15th century manor house and a £250k modern office refurbishment.
